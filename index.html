About Me

Personal Life

Born and raised in Kampala, Eric King spent most of his childhood listening to raggae songs, rock band and local music. What took his attention and inspired his muse were songs by the West Life band. At an early age King was playing guitar and piano. he later joined Kampala Musical School where his serious carreer in music started.

Music Career

Even before Eric could with the strings of the guitar he had started miming songs by West Life. At the age of 15, he realised he could write his own songs and it didnot take him long before he penned the song Sweet love followed by paffu not long after. In 2017, Eric King hit the studio to produce his debut album that hit airwaves allover Uganda
